Meet Abbey

Abigail Aaron is an artist who has currently graduated with BA (hons) degree in Fine Art at Leeds Arts University. She focuses predominantly on abstraction, using acrylic paint and pastels as her main mediums. Abiagil's art is form of visual storytelling interested in conveying spirituality through paint by colour lines, layers and mark making. Inspired by spirituality and religious texts she has an interest in an abstract narrative and creates paintings based on them.

Her work is a personal reflection on spirituality through exploring her faith and turning scripture into abstract paintings.

She chooses to communicate her interpretations through layers of overlapping acrylic paint, charcoal and mixed media expressing abstract mark making through colour and line. Each mark represents scripture and spirituality in a way that can evoke an emotional response.
